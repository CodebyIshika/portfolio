About Me

I'm Ishika, a budding software developer currently pursuing my software developer diploma at the Manitoba Institute of Trades and Technology (MITT).My journey into the world of software development was sparked by a moment of curiosity and the desire to turn lines of code into tangible, impactful creations. Intrigued by this newfound world, I embarked on a mission armed with a beginner's guide and an unwavering determination to unravel the secrets of the digital realm. Three months into this coding odyssey at MITT, I've dived into various programming languages, embraced coding challenges, and discovered the thrill of problem-solving. The ever-evolving nature of the tech world keeps me on my toes, and I love every moment of this exhilarating ride.

Beyond the lines of code and the world of software development, I find solace and inspiration in a palette of diverse hobbies. Drawing has been my creative outlet, a space where I bring imagination to life with strokes of colors and forms. Nature exploration is my therapy, a grounding experience that allows me to connect with the beauty of the outdoors. These hobbies, drawing, nature exploration, and music listening, not only offer a well-rounded balance to my life but also influence my approach to problem-solving and creativity in the digital realm.
